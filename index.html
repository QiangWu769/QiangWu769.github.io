About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University at Buffalo (SUNY), advised by Prof. Yaxiong Xie.

Before joining UB, I received a bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Peking University and a bachelor’s degree in Chinese Language and Literature from Wuhan University.
